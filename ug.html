It was founded in 1948 as the University of Gold Coast and its colleges were based with the association of the university in London. The school is in five basic colleges which are Basic and applied science, Humanities, Education and Health science.

University of Ghana is made up of Undergraduate and Graduate students who fall under the following schools or department depending on the person's course.

Basic and applied science has various schools including School of Agriculture, School of Biological Sciences, School of Engineering, School of Nuclear and Allied Sciences, School of Physical and Mathematical Sciences, School of Veterinary Medicine

The institute of the Basic and Applied Science are Institute of Environment, and Sanitation Studies, Institute of Applied Science and Technology, Biotechnology Centre and The West African Centre for Crop Improvement. With five undergraduate and six graduate schools having thirty-one (31) and sixty-three departments respectively.

Under Humanities, we have six schools which includes university of Ghana Business school, school of performing Arts school of social sciences, school of performing arts school pf law and school of languages.

Health Sciences also consist of six schools some which are school of pharmacy, school of public health, the dental school, and others.

Under Education we have three major schools under which departments fall under, these are the school of Continuing and Distance Education, school of Information and Communication Studies and School of Education and Leadership.

Over time university of Ghana has had various heads which includes; Conor Cruise O'Brien (1962-1965), Alexander Adom Kwapong (1966-1975), Akilapa Sawyer (1992-1996), George Benneh (1992-1996), Ivan Addae- Mensah (1996-2002), Kwadwo Asenso-Okyere (2002-2006), Clifford Nii-Boi Tagoe (2006-2010), Ernest Aryeetey (2010-2016) and Ebenezer Oduro Owusu (2016-2021)

It currently has Mrs. Mary Chinery-Hesse, Justice Sophia A. B Akuffo and Prof.NanaAba Appiah Amfo serving as Chancellor, Chairperson and Vice Chancellor, respectively. The former vice chancellor being Mrs. Ebenezer Oduro Owusu and the former chancellor being Mr. Kofi Anan.
